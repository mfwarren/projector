Projector

Estimating how much time a project will take has never been easy. Projector is designed to make it easy to create the most accurate estimates possible.

Features:

  • Mitigate anchoring bias
  • Includes uncertainty in the estimate
  • collect and combine estimates from many people
  • automatically include estimates from similar tasks on past projects
  • Utilize reference class forecasting to mitigate optimism bias
